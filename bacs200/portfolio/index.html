About Me

Born in Loveland Colorado into an artistic family, I've always been fascinated by the combitnation of thourough logical thinking with aesthetic merit. I was intrigued by the practical process of making massive artistic endeavors like movies and plays and would seek out the knowledge on how to do such things.

This thirst for knowledge led me to explore things like writing music, programming, and web development.

After discovering that these artistic endeavors also require large amounts of logical reasoning and problem solving I decided to make them my careers. I've poured hours of my free time into developing my skills in these fields along with studying web development and software engineering at the University of Northern Colorado in Greeley Colorado.
