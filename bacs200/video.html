Boys to Men Mentoring strives to build communities of great male role models for teanage boys. In a world where the idea of masculinity and manliness feels negative and daunting, Boys to Men offers male leaders who can teach our teenage boys how to be good, strong, emotional, successful, and powerful men. Redefining what it means to be strong, Boys to Men provides mentors to thousands of teens, many of whom grow up with absent fathers, with a curriculum that incorporates holistic, intergenerational, and asset-based mentoring practices and encourages community involvement, intensive self-reflection, positive critical thinking habits, and self-accountability.

There are many ways to contribute to Boys to Men. Donating directly is greatly appreciated, especially during these trying times. However, you can also become a member. With varying levels of membeerships, you can donate monthly and in return you will receive discounted or complimentary admission to all BTM sponsored events, quarterly updates on boys and other success stories, printed version of our annual report, invitation to the annual Members Meeting, along with other various benefits depending on the level of your membership.

For those who want to be more involved in the program, the option to become a mentor is also avaliable. If you are interested or wish to know more about what it takes to become a Boys to Men mentor email mentor coordinator Marco Rodriguez at marco@boystomen.org or call our office at 619-469-9599.
